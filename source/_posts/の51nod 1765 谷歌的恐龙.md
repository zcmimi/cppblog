其实下面给出的$a$数组没什么鸟用

列出方程：

$E = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \frac 1n \times ( i+ flag \times E)$

解方程：
$$
E = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \frac 1n \times ( i+ flag \times E)
\\\\
E = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \frac in + (1 - \frac mn)E 
\\\\
\frac mnE = \frac {\frac 12n(n-1)}n
\\\\
E = \frac {n(n-1)}{2m}
$$
