$$
\overbrace{111\dots111}^{\text{N个1}} \equiv k \pmod m
\\
\frac{10^N-1}9\equiv k \pmod m
\\
10^N\equiv 9k+1 \pmod m
$$

顺利地推出是bsgs,($m$是质数,也就不用exbsgs了)
